PrepPal - Meal Prep Made Simple
===============================


Welcome to PrepPal
------------------

**A minimal, stress-free solution** for organizing your meals and recipes.  
We built PrepPal to remove the complexity from meal planning, helping you:  

🍽 **Save time** with quick recipe logging  
🛒 **Reduce waste** with clear ingredient tracking  
⏱ **Simplify decisions** with at-a-glance prep times  

Our Philosophy
--------------

*"Good tools disappear when you use them."*  

PrepPal focuses on what matters:  
✓ **No clutter** – Just the essentials  
✓ **No learning curve** – Instantly familiar  
✓ **No distractions** – Pure meal planning  


We are trying to make an application that will live and keep helping people who suffer to find a great application that is specialized in meal prepping!
